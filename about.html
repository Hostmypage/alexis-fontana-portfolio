About me!

Hello! I'm Alexis, graphic designer and illustrator currently immersed in the world of editorial work. My artistic journey began with me drawing for classmates and incorporating my skills into school projects, not only as a creative outlet but also as a way to make learning more engaging and enjoyable for everyone

My skill set

My time studying Visual Communication Design has given me the chance to learn the use of various programs such as Illustrator, Photoshop, Paint tool Sai, AutoCad, InDesign and basic understanding of HTML, CSS and JavaScript. Beyond creating beautiful illustrations, I specialize in crafting visuals that communicate messages with clarity and impact. Whether it's through editorial illustrations, branding visuals, or immersive storytelling, I approach each project with a keen understanding of the communicative power of art. Versatility is at the core of my practice. From traditional mediums to digital illustration, I navigate a spectrum of tools to bring ideas to life. This flexibility allows me to adapt my style to suit the narrative and engage diverse audiences.

My background

I am currently pursuing a degree in Visual Communication Design at the UNLP(national university of La Plata), where I am honing my skills and expanding my creative horizons. The academic environment has been an incredible source of inspiration, allowing me to experiment with various design principles and techniques while incorporating my illustration skills developed through courses and being self taught.

A little about my ambitions

As a children's illustrator my ambitions extend beyond the illustrations themselves. I aspire to craft enchanting worlds that not only entertain but also instill valuable lessons and ignite curiosity in the minds of a given public. I am dedicated to creating illustrations that resonate with both children and the young at heart.
